QDCG QUANTUM CIRCUIT
==================================================

Qubit Mapping:
  Qubit 0 = CAT
  Qubit 1 = SIT
  Qubit 2 = CHAIR

Circuit Diagram:
Circuit(QDCG_reasoning, qubits=3, gates=12)

Operations:
  1. H(0), H(1), H(2) - Put all qubits in superposition
  2. CX(0,1) - CNOT from CAT to SIT
  3. RY(1, 1.34) - Rotate SIT by 0.85*pi/2 (encodes edge weight)
  4. CX(0,1) - CNOT from CAT to SIT
  5. CX(1,2) - CNOT from SIT to CHAIR
  6. RY(2, 1.41) - Rotate CHAIR by 0.90*pi/2 (encodes edge weight)
  7. CX(1,2) - CNOT from SIT to CHAIR
  8. H(0), H(1), H(2) - Create interference
  9. Measure all qubits
